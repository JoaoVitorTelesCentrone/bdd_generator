DESCRIÇÃO:
### Summary

On ones own blog post, while one can delete a comment residing on said post, one can not delete a reply to a comment; although one can do so on ones own status post.

### Steps to reproduce

Go to one of your blog posts. Find a reply to your comment. Click the 3 vertical dots, and the option to delete a reply is not present; as it is in a status post.

### Platform information 

Firefox 60.5.1 ESR; Debian 'Stretch' Linux 6.8

### What is the current *bug* behavior?

The vertical dots opening the options menu on comments/replies on ones blog post do not show the option to delete, as it does on a status post.

### What is the expected *correct* behavior?

The vertical dots opening the options menu on comments/replies on ones blog post SHOULD show the option to delete, as it does on a status post.
