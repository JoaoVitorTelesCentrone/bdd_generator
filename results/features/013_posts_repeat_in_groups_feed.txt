We have a few reports of posts repeating in groups. [This Gitlab issue](https://gitlab.com/minds/front/-/issues/5172) makes reference to the issue in a test Group. Additionally, a user alerted me that [this Group](https://www.minds.com/groups/profile/569272650258460672/feed) exhibits the issue, though notably the posts repeat only after scrolling VERY deep into the timeline (~100+ posts).
