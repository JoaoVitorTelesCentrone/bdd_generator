## Acceptance

### Interaction: Channel has activity posts

- Given the user is viewing a Channel page,
- and the channel page DOES have activity posts in its feed,
- when the user clicks to subscribe to that channel,
- then the channel recommendations component appears at the top of the activity post feed,
- and the heading for the component reads `Consider subscribing to`,
- and the recommended channels are sourced from the logic described below.

NOTE: This is not a functional requirement where the channel feed is empty, as recommendations will be displayed by default (see below)

- [See prototype here](https://www.figma.com/proto/c37p1Jz209IT8vRuc1AoFv/Recommendations?page-id=0%3A1&node-id=72%3A847&viewport=241%2C48%2C1&scaling=scale-down&starting-point-node-id=72%3A847&show-proto-sidebar=1)


### Interaction: Channel does not have activity posts

- Given the user is viewing a Channel page,
- and the channel page DOES NOT have activity posts in its feed,
- when the page is loaded,
- then the channel recommendations component appears at the top of the activity post feed,
- and the heading for the component reads `Consider subscribing to`,
- and the recommended channels are sourced from the logic described below.

If the user were to subscribe, the recommendation slide in (described earlier) would not display.

### Which channels are recommended (logic)

- Given the channel DOES have subscriptions to other channels...
  - Include: Channels subscribed to by the channel the user is currently viewing
  - Filter out: Channels the user already subscribes to.
  - Order by: Prioritize channels by (1) mutual subscribers, (2) total subscribers, and (3) having activity on Minds within the past 7 days.
---
- Given the channel DOES NOT have subscriptions to other channels...
  - Include: Channels subscribed to by @Minds account.
  - Filter out: Channels the user already subscribes to.
  - Order by: Prioritize channels by (1) mutual subscribers, (2) total subscribers, and (3) having activity on Minds within the past 7 days.
